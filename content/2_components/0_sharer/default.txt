Title: Sharer

----

Content:

Opens a social media sharer window with prefilled URL and text or copies the page URL to the clipboard.

(example: sharer active: result)

## Options

### `type`

Which social media sharer to invoke:

- [`facebook`](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/plugins/share-button)
- [`twitter`](https://developer.twitter.com/en/docs/twitter-for-websites/web-intents/overview)
- [`linkedin`](https://stackoverflow.com/a/33746543/3130281)
- [`reddit`](https://stackoverflow.com/a/24851347/3130281)
- [`pinterest`](https://stackoverflow.com/a/11212220/3130281)

Can also be set to `copy` to copy the URL to the clipboard instead.

**Default:** `undefined`

### `url`

What URL to share.

**Default:** `window.location.href`

### `text`

What text to share. Only available for `twitter`, `reddit`, and `pinterest`.

**Default:** `document.title`

### `image`

What image to share. Only available for `pinterest`.

**Default:** `undefined`

### `width`

Width of the opened popup window.

**Default:** `600`

### `height`

Height of the opened popup window.

**Default:** `400`
